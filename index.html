Calvin Leung

As of the time of this writing, I am a National Defense Science and Engineering Graduate Fellow and a rising fifth-year PhD candidate at MIT in the Department of Physics. I am a member of the CHIME/FRB Collaboration, and my advisor is Kiyo Masui. My research program centers on the observation of fast radio bursts (FRBs) and developing techniques to use them as probes of the large scale structure of the universe.
